The game consists of 4 sequential modules that must be completed in order. Each module presents a unique challenge that tests different skills:

1. Elf Crisis

Help the elves fix broken toys before Christmas! This is a puzzle/action game where you need to repair toys.

2. Reindeer Navigation

Guide Santa's reindeer through obstacles and challenges. Test your reflexes and navigation skills!

3. Gift Ethics

Make important decisions about gift distribution. Your choices matter in this ethical challenge!

4. Emotion Stabilizer

Spread joy and happiness to different regions of the world. Balance emotions to ensure a happy Christmas!
